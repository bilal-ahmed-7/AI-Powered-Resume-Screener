About AI-Powered Resume Screener

Project Overview

The AI-Powered Resume Screener is an advanced application designed to streamline the hiring process by automatically analyzing resumes and matching them with job descriptions using artificial intelligence. Our platform helps job seekers find the most suitable positions for their skills and assists employers in identifying the best candidates efficiently.

Our Mission

Our mission is to revolutionize the recruitment process by leveraging AI technology to create more accurate, efficient, and unbiased resume screening. We aim to save time for both job seekers and employers while improving the quality of job matches.

Technology Stack

Backend

  • Python
  • Flask Web Framework
  • SQLAlchemy ORM
  • PostgreSQL Database
  • Natural Language Processing (NLP)

Frontend

  • HTML5, CSS3, JavaScript
  • Bootstrap 5
  • Jinja2 Templates
  • AJAX for asynchronous requests

Key Features

  • AI-powered resume analysis and scoring
  • Automated skill extraction and matching
  • Role-based access control (Admin/Client)
  • Resume management and version tracking
  • Job listing and application management
  • Detailed analytics and reporting

Development Team

Our application was developed by a team of skilled software engineers, data scientists, and UI/UX designers passionate about creating innovative solutions for the recruitment industry.
